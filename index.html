Golf Courses around Ireland

Course Types:

There are two main types of golf courses in Ireland - links courses and parkland courses.

How many courses?

There are over 400 courses scattered all over Ireland to choose from.

Reasons to play in Ireland

No 1:

Ireland has many of the world's best links golf courses.

No 2:

You will experience warm and welcoming hospitality from all of our Hotels and will always find a bit of craic in the local Irish pub.

Why choose Ireland to play golf?

Ireland's golf courses are surrounded by nature with scenic views that have been moulded beautifully by golf architects. Their golf courses are famous among golfers all over the world for their fine golfing challenges in the midst of breath-taking scenery. That is probably one of the reasons why Ireland is known as the 'golfer's paradise'. If planned with the right travel agent, you can get one such golfing trip at an affordable rate.


Ireland is home to over 400 golf clubs, including a third of the world's natural links courses, and a selection of exceptional championship courses in amazing locations, so it's no wonder that every year over 240,000 golfers from all over the world come to the island of Ireland's greens to experience the impressive but unforgiving fairways.
